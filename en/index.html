INFECTION SURVEILLANCE ASSISTANT - ISA

The ISA-robot is a machine learning algorithm that simulates the work of the infection control specialists in healthcare-associated infections surveillance. The ML algorithm has high sensitivity and accuracy for the classification of healthcare-associated infections.

Services

SURVEILLANCE OF HOSPITAL INFECTIONS


The accurate classification of healthcare-associated infections, the process of planning for safe practices, and deployment of safer care are implemented by local and remote teams. Infection safety is monitored by monthly indicators reviewed by both teams.

TELECONSULTING


Our team (infectious diseases physicians, nurses and pharmacists) offers real-time and asynchronous consulting for healthcare professionals in hospitals, clinics or offices. From 7 a.m. to 11 p.m., seven days a week.

MOBILE SURVEILLANCE (Bundle App)


Tool that allows infection control staff to collect bundles indicators using a mobile device, integrated with our web platform.

ISA ROBOT


The infection surveillance assistance is a machine learning algorithm that simulates what infection control professionals do in healthcare-associated infections surveillance. In a semiautomated method the humans confirm (or not) the algorithm classification.

TELEMONITORING OF ANTIMICROBIALS (TELESTEWARDSHIP)


Infectious disease specialists respond asynchronous or in real-time, from 7 a.m. to 11 p.m., seven days a week. For every antimicrobial prescribed or included in the system a second opinion by a specialist is added for decision-making purposes.

TELECONSULTATION CHATBOTS


Digital assistants with human curation that enable quick and intuitive consultations to routines and guidelines and provides real-time contact with Qualis experts.
