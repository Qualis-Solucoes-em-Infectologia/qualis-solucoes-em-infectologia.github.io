About Us

We are a group of specialists in infectious diseases and infection control and work to promote the safety of patients, healthcare workers, and environments using technology and innovation. Our services and products are designed as telemedicine tools for infectious diseases, hospital infection control, epidemiology of hospital infections, and antimicrobial stewardship. Our multidisciplinary team has the expertise and scientific knowledge in infectious diseases, hospital infection, and technology. We provide tools and guide work processes through a connected and safe network.

How does it work?

Qualis proposes and participates in a change in work processes using expertise and technology. Quality improvement methodology such as the PDSA (Plan, Do, Study, Act), real-time and asynchronous antimicrobial stewardship, and hospital infection surveillance with artificial intelligence (ISA-robot) are some of the products and services offered by the Qualis team. The rearrangement of infection control services based on a chain with rules, standards, and contingencies, improves efficiency and quality and creates safety mechanisms for people and environments.
